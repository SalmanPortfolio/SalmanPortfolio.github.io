Finals Preparation and Challenges
1. Post-Meds Struggles

After completing the Meds period, I focused on covering remaining subjects while preparing for the final exams. The initial weeks were challenging because my prior knowledge was limited and I had missed important concepts during earlier classes. Nights were spent studying, revising lectures, and trying to solve past papers. The lack of a personal laptop made digital resources harder to access, adding to time pressure.

2. Final Exams Preparation

As the final exam schedule approached, I intensified my preparation. Staying up late and managing time became essential. Despite challenges in understanding some topics, I managed to consolidate knowledge from Meds and other semester lectures. The combination of previous stress, limited resources, and the high stakes of finals made this period one of the most demanding of the semester.

3. Results and Reflection

Upon completion of finals, I reflected on the effort and sacrifices made throughout the semester. While not all exam outcomes were ideal, I was relieved to have passed. This experience taught me valuable lessons in time management, prioritization, and resilience in the face of personal and academic challenges.
