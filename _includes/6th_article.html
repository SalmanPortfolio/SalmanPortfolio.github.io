Facing Academic Challenges and Midterm Pressure
1. Struggles with Quiz Patterns

During the sixth week, our academic journey started becoming more serious. We began facing quizzes, but I had no clear understanding of their pattern. This created confusion because university-level quizzes were very different from what I had experienced before. The questions required conceptual understanding rather than simple memorization. Due to this lack of preparation and awareness, I faced many difficulties and could not perform well. This phase made me realize that I needed to change my study approach and focus more on understanding concepts deeply.

2. Midterm Preparation Challenges

After the quizzes, midterm exams started approaching, which increased the pressure even more. Coming from a Pre-Medical background, subjects like mathematics and programming were already difficult for me. I had no prior experience in these areas, so everything felt new and challenging. I spent long nights studying and trying to complete my syllabus, but still, I was unable to cover everything. Time management became a major issue, and I often felt stressed because some topics remained incomplete. Despite putting in effort, I felt that my preparation was not enough.

3. Learning from Difficult Experiences

When the midterm exams finally started, I faced several challenges. Some papers did not go well because I was still struggling to fully understand the concepts. Although my performance was not as good as I expected, this experience taught me an important lesson. I realized that hard work alone is not enough; I needed to study smartly and focus on understanding rather than memorizing. This difficult phase helped me identify my weaknesses and motivated me to improve in the future.
